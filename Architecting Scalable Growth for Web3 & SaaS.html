About Me

Im Kamila Lipska, a full-stack growth architect with 15+ years of experience in SaaS, Web3, and digital ecosystems. I specialize in designing scalable marketing infrastructures that connect strategy with implementation, enabling high-velocity growth for companies navigating complexity, fragmentation, or rapid expansion.

From marketing data pipelines to automation architecture, attribution modeling to GTM strategy, I dont just identify gaps, I build the systems that fix them for good. CEOs and CMOs trust me when they need a senior operator who can move fast, solve without hand-holding, and lead complex initiatives across brands, teams, or toolsets.

Im EU-based, operate globally, and thrive in environments where autonomy, clarity, and strategic depth matter. My approach is grounded in analytics, driven by impact, and built for scale. I work remotely with American tech companies and founders who need someone they dont have to micromanage.

My Approach

I architect growth systems that unify strategy with execution by blending precision, speed, and data integrity across the stack. My work goes beyond campaigns: I design self-sustaining infrastructures that turn fragmented tools and processes into revenue-aligned engines. From first-party data pipelines to lifecycle automation and performance funnels, everything I build is designed to remove guesswork, scale efficiently, and drive repeatable growth.

If it doesnt generate impact or unlock leverage, it doesnt belong in the system.

Im a hybrid operator, strategic enough to architect the entire growth stack, technical enough to implement it without friction.
