About Me

Strategic depth meets technical execution

I'm Kamila Lipska, a full-stack growth architect with 15+ years of experience in SaaS, Web3, and digital ecosystems. I specialize in designing scalable marketing infrastructures that connect strategy with implementation, enabling high-velocity growth for companies navigating complexity, fragmentation, or rapid expansion.

From marketing data pipelines to automation architecture, attribution modeling to GTM strategy, I don't just identify gaps, I build the systems that fix them for good. CEOs and CMOs trust me when they need a senior operator who can move fast, solve without hand-holding, and lead complex initiatives across brands, teams, or toolsets.

I'm EU-based, operate globally, and thrive in environments where autonomy, clarity, and strategic depth matter. My approach is grounded in analytics, driven by impact, and built for scale. I work remotely with American tech companies and founders who need someone they don't have to micromanage.
