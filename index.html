I'm not your typical marketer. I'm a systems thinker who sees the bigger picture and builds infrastructure that scales.

Expert

PostHog
Expert

I use PostHog to map real user behavior and make sense of complex customer journeys. From event-based tracking to funnel breakdowns and cohort segmentation, I turn behavioral data into clear growth insights. My implementations help uncover where users drop off, how they interact with key flows, and what patterns signal intent.

Hotjar
Advanced
